# DuckyA Privacy Policy
Last updated: 2026-05-26

DuckyA stores your phone number, Firebase user ID, and chat
messages in Google Firebase to provide messaging and call functionality.

We do NOT sell or share your data with third parties.
We use Firebase Authentication, Firestore, Storage, Cloud Messaging,
and Analytics — all governed by Google's privacy policy.
